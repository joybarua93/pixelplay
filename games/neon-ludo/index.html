  • The Magic 6 Roll a 6 to deploy a puck. 6s grant a bonus roll!

  • Boost Pads Land on a purple pad to instantly jump +4 spaces and gain a Surge Ring.
  • Slingshot KO Land on an enemy to capture them. You jump +6 spaces AND get a bonus roll!
